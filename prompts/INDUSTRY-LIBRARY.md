## MODULE 01 — RETAIL & E-COMMERCE

**Triggers:** shop, store, buy, cart, ecommerce, shopify, product, sale, shipping
**Margin benchmark:** 40-65% gross margin
**Repurchase cycle:** 30-90 days
**Platform priority:** Instagram → Pinterest → TikTok → Facebook

**Content angles:**
- Product showcase & styling
- Behind-the-scenes packing/fulfilment
- Customer unboxing & reviews
- Sale announcements & urgency
- "How to style" tutorials
- New arrivals & drops
- Abandoned cart recovery hooks
- Seasonal campaigns (EOFY, Christmas, BFCM)

**Top hashtag groups:**
- Broad: #onlineshopping #shopsmall #retailtherapy
- Niche: #[product-category] #shoplocal #[brandname]store
- Conversion: #sale #newstock #limitedstock

**SEO targets:** "[product] buy online", "[product] Australia", "best [product] for [use-case]"

**Platform priority:** LinkedIn → Facebook → Google Business → YouTube

- Industry news commentary
- FAQ: answers to common client questions

## MODULE 12 — HEALTHCARE & MEDICAL

**Triggers:** medical, clinic, doctor, dentist, physiotherapy, allied health, hospital
**Margin benchmark:** Varies widely by service type
**Repurchase cycle:** 3-6 months (ongoing care), annual (checkups)
**Platform priority:** Facebook → Google Business → Instagram → LinkedIn

**Content angles:**
- Patient education (condition explanations)
- Team introductions & qualifications
- FAQ videos answering common questions
- Health tips by season
- New service/technology announcements
- Community health events
- Appointment reminder hooks
- "Myth vs fact" content

**COMPLIANCE NOTE:** All healthcare content must comply with TGA/AHPRA guidelines. No before/after claims without proper consent and qualifying statements. No testimonials that imply guaranteed outcomes.

## MODULE 14 — FINANCIAL SERVICES

**Triggers:** finance, wealth, investment, super, tax, mortgage, loan, insurance, broker
**Margin benchmark:** 1-3% AUM or fixed fee structures
**Repurchase cycle:** Annual reviews, ongoing advisory
**Platform priority:** LinkedIn → Facebook → YouTube → Instagram

**Content angles:**
- "X financial mistakes people make in their 30s/40s"
- Market commentary & economic updates
- Tax planning tips by season
- Client milestone stories (retirement, first home, etc.)
- Plain-English explainers of complex topics
- Team expertise & credentials
- Regulatory updates that affect clients

**COMPLIANCE NOTE:** All financial advice content requires appropriate disclaimers per ASIC regulations. General advice vs personal advice distinction must be clear.
