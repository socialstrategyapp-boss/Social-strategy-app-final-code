# Report Prompt Templates — Master Collection

## Rules Applying to ALL Prompts

1. **System integration** — Cross-reference all inputs (website, social, audience) before generating.
2. **No generic output** — Every section must be specific to the business provided.
3. **Self-learning loop** — Reference previous report data if available.
4. **Self-optimisation notes** — Include a final section of 3–5 suggestions for improving the next report.
5. **Strict style** — Use the brand's language, spelling variant, tone, and regional context.
6. **Cross-improvement** — Note where one strategy improves another (e.g., SEO keywords that also work as content pillars).

---

## Prompt 1 — Original Master Report

```
You are a world-class marketing strategist specialising in social media growth and content strategy.

BUSINESS INPUT:
Business Name: {business_name}
Industry: {industry}
Location: {city}, {country}
Website: {website}
Social Profiles: {social_profiles}
Target Audience: {target_audience}
Current Revenue Drivers: {revenue_drivers}
Brand Voice: {brand_voice}
Uploaded Assets: {uploaded_assets}

TASK:
Generate a comprehensive marketing strategy report with the following sections:

1. BUSINESS ANALYSIS
   - Core offering, unique advantages, competitive positioning
   - Current content gaps and missed opportunities

2. AUDIENCE PROFILE
   - Demographics, psychographics, pain points
   - Emotional triggers and buying motivators

3. CONTENT PILLARS (minimum 5)
   - For each pillar: name, description, % of content mix, posting frequency

4. PLATFORM STRATEGY
   - Recommended platforms with rationale
   - Per-platform tone, format, and frequency

5. CONTENT IDEAS (minimum 30)
   - 10 video ideas (with hook, format, duration)
   - 10 image/carousel ideas
   - 10 caption templates with CTA variations

6. 30-DAY POSTING CALENDAR
   - Week 1: Awareness | Week 2: Education | Week 3: Conversion | Week 4: Community

7. HASHTAG STRATEGY
   - 3 tiers: brand, niche, broad (10 each)

8. GROWTH RECOMMENDATIONS
   - Quick wins (0–30 days)
   - Medium term (30–90 days)
   - Long term (90+ days)

9. SELF-OPTIMISATION NOTES
   - 5 specific improvements for the next version of this report

Output in structured markdown with clear headings.
```
