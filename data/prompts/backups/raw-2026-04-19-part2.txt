## Universal Content Modules (Applies to All Industries)

### Weekly Schedule Template

| Day | Theme | Format | Purpose |
|---|---|---|---|
| Monday | Offer/Product | Reel or Image | Drive awareness of top offer |
| Tuesday | Education | Carousel or Text | Build authority & value |
| Wednesday | Behind the Scenes | Reel | Build trust & personality |
| Thursday | Feature/Product | Image or Video | Second offer highlight |
| Friday | Conversion | Reel + Caption | Direct CTA |
| Saturday | UGC / Repost | Story or Image | Social proof |
| Sunday | Lifestyle / Inspiration | Image or Reel | Emotional connection |

### Monthly Rotation

| Week | Focus | Content Type |
|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | Awareness | Hook-heavy, discovery, trend-adjacent |
| Week 2 | Education | Value-dense, tutorials, authority |
| Week 3 | Conversion | Offers, testimonials, urgency |
| Week 4 | Community | Engagement, UGC, retention |

### 14-Day Recycle Rule
Every 14 days: take top 3 performing posts and re-post with:
- New hook (different type)
- New angle (different emotion)
- Same core message

### Idea Multiplier Framework
For every 1 idea, generate 8 variations:
1. Question hook version
2. Bold statement version
3. Transformation angle
4. Social proof angle
5. Video script
6. 5-slide carousel outline
7. Beginner version
8. Expert version
